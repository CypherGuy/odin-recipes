Fish & Chips

Ingredients:

  • White fish fillets (such as cod or haddock)
  • Potatoes
  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Peel and cut potatoes into thick strips for chips.
  2. Soak the potato strips in cold water for 30 minutes, then pat them dry.
  3. Preheat oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (180°C).
  4. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and salt to make the batter.
  5. Dip fish fillets in the batter, then carefully place them in the hot oil.
  6. Fry fish until golden brown and crispy, then remove and drain on paper towels.
  7. Fry the potato strips until golden and crispy.
  8. Serve fish and chips hot with tartar sauce, lemon wedges, and optional malt vinegar.
