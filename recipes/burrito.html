Burrito

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oreg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 (16 ounce) can refried beans
  • 4 (10 inch) flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 2 cups chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, brown beef and onion over medium heat. Drain off grease, and season with garlic powder, cumin, oregano, salt and pepper.
  2. Spread beans on each tortilla. Spoon beef mixture down the center of each tortilla. Sprinkle with cheese, lettuce, tomatoes and green onions.
  3. Fold up the sides of the tortillas, and then roll to enclose the filling. Serve hot.
