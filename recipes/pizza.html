Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 3 1/2 cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup cornmeal
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup sliced mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup sliced red onion
  • 1/2 cup black olives
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup cooked crumbled sausage

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, salt and 2 tablespoons olive oil. Stir in the yeast mixture. When the dough has pulled together, turn it out onto a lightly floured surface and knead until smooth, about 8 minutes.
  3. Lightly oil a large bowl, place the dough in the bowl and turn to coat with oil. Cover with a damp cloth and let it rise in a warm place until doubled in volume, about 1 hour.
  4. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). If you are baking the pizza on a pizza stone, you may place your toppings on the dough, and bake immediately. If you are baking your pizza in a pan, lightly oil the pan, and let the dough rise for 15 or 20 minutes before topping and baking it.
  5. Roll dough with a rolling pin, and transfer to a lightly greased pizza pan or baker's peel dusted with cornmeal. Spread with pizza sauce. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese. Arrange desired toppings over mozzarella cheese, and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes, or until golden brown. Let baked pizza cool for 5 minutes before serving.
